The Agenda · 2027

A Vision for
Mangu/Bokkos

Eight clear priorities. Shaped by three decades of national service. Built around the real needs, real challenges, and real aspirations of our constituency.

Why This Agenda

Priorities Built
on Listening

's vision for Mangu/Bokkos is not a politician's checklist. It is a structured agenda shaped by decades of managing national systems, listening to communities, and understanding what genuine representation at the National Assembly can unlock for a constituency.

Our constituency deserves a leader who arrives in Abuja knowing exactly what to fight for — and having the credibility, discipline, and connections to deliver it.

These eight priorities are that agenda. Not vague promises. A clear programme.

Eight Pillars

The Programme

01 🛡️
Security & Lasting Peace

Security is the foundation on which everything else is built. Without it, farming is dangerous, businesses cannot grow, and children cannot attend school safely. AVM Masiyer brings a military strategist's understanding of security — and will use his National Assembly platform to drive structural solutions to the farmer-herder crisis.

Engage federal security institutions with firsthand knowledge of their capabilities Facilitate dialogue between farming and herding communities at the national level Advocate for improved security deployment and early warning systems Support community policing and local security infrastructure through federal budgets
02 🌾
Farming & Local Business Support

Mangu/Bokkos is a farming constituency. Its people deserve a representative who will fight for them in the halls where federal agricultural policy is made — from crop insurance to storage infrastructure to market access.

Advocate for federal agricultural programmes targeting small-scale farmers Push for post-harvest storage and rural market infrastructure Support access to affordable farm inputs through federal agencies Champion local business development and cooperative structures
03 🎓
Quality Education & Healthcare

Every child in Mangu/Bokkos deserves access to quality education. Every family deserves functional healthcare. AVM Masiyer will work to direct federal allocations toward school infrastructure, trained teachers, health centres, and essential medicines.

Engage UBEC and TETFUND for school construction and rehabilitation Advocate for Primary Healthcare Centre upgrades across wards Support teacher training and deployment programmes Push for constituency-specific health outreach campaigns
04 🌟
Youth Empowerment & Employment

The young people of Mangu/Bokkos are talented, driven, and deserving of real opportunity. AVM Masiyer is committed to connecting them to federal skill acquisition programmes, entrepreneurship funding, and employment creation initiatives.

Direct federal skill acquisition centres to the constituency Engage NPOWER, YOUWIN, and similar federal schemes for local beneficiaries Support youth-owned SMEs through CBN and BOI programmes Advocate for youth inclusion in federal appointments and scholarship schemes
05 🏗️
Economic & Physical Development

Infrastructure is the enabler of all economic activity. Roads, electricity, water, and connectivity are not luxuries — they are prerequisites for a constituency that can grow. AVM Masiyer will fight for Mangu/Bokkos in every federal budget cycle.

Use constituency project allocations strategically and transparently Engage FERMA, REC, and federal ministries for road rehabilitation Advocate for REA rural electrification and RUWASSA water projects Facilitate FDI and public-private partnership opportunities for the constituency
06 👩‍👧‍👦
Women, Youth, Elderly & the Vulnerable

True representation means no one is left behind. AVM Masiyer is committed to targeted empowerment for women, elderly citizens, people living with disability, and the most vulnerable in our communities — ensuring they benefit from federal programmes designed to serve them.

Ensure women and elderly groups benefit from TraderMoni and similar federal schemes Advocate for disability-inclusive federal programme delivery in the constituency Support social protection and safety net programmes at the local level Champion legislative reforms that protect women and vulnerable populations
07 🏛️
Cultural Heritage & Societal Engagement

Mangu/Bokkos has a rich, proud, and distinct cultural identity. AVM Masiyer will champion positive societal engagement — fostering cultural pride within our constituency and projecting it outward to the national and international stage.

Support cultural festivals and heritage preservation initiatives Advocate for cultural tourism development in the constituency Engage NCAC and federal cultural bodies for constituency programmes Ensure the voices of traditional institutions are represented at the federal level
08 🗣️
A Powerful Voice at the National Stage

This is the overarching commitment: that when important federal decisions are made, the name Mangu/Bokkos is heard. AVM Masiyer's military rank, his professional relationships, and his credibility give this constituency a representative who walks into any room with authority.

Leverage national networks from a 35-year military career for constituency benefit Ensure Mangu/Bokkos is represented in key House committees Use the floor of the House to speak for the security and development needs of our people Build cross-party relationships that deliver results, not just rhetoric
