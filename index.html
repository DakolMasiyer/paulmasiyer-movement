The Man Behind the Rank

Leadership
Forged in Service

Air Vice Marshal Paul D. Masiyer (Rtd.) is a distinguished son of Mangu/Bokkos Federal Constituency, Plateau State. Over more than three decades in the Nigerian Air Force, he rose to its peak — trusted with the nation's most critical defence responsibilities.

Having served as Chief of Logistics, HQ NAF and as Commandant of the Military Training Centre, he brings a systems-thinker's discipline and a leader's accountability to everything he undertakes.
