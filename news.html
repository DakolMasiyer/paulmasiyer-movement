Campaign Update 2025 · Mangu/Bokkos, Plateau State

(Rtd.) Announces Candidacy for Mangu/Bokkos House of Representatives Seat

With great pride and hope for a brighter future, Air Vice Marshal Paul Masiyer (Rtd.) formally enters the political landscape of Mangu/Bokkos Federal Constituency — a Plateau son, bringing 35 years of national service back home.

Having risen to the peak of military service as Air Vice Marshal — serving as Chief of Logistics at Headquarters Nigerian Air Force and as Commandant of the Military Training Centre — AVM Masiyer now turns his experience, discipline, and strategic leadership to the service of his own constituency under the platform of the All Progressives Congress (APC).
